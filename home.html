Welcome to ClubSphere

Unleash your creativity, explore your interests, and make your college life more vibrant. Join, manage, and participate in events across all your favorite clubs!

Spoorthi - The Literacy Club

Mission: Encourage expression through debates, writing, and oratory events. Cultivate language and leadership skills.

Events: Essay Writing, Debate, Storytelling, Extempore.

Eligibility: Open to all students interested in language, literature, and communication.

Prakruthi - Environmental Club

Mission: Promote sustainability and environmental awareness. Empower students to lead eco-friendly initiatives.

Events: Tree Planting, Clean-up Drives, Recycling Campaigns, Environmental Talks.

Eligibility: Open to students who are passionate about nature and ecology.

Kruthi - Fine Arts Club

Mission: Provide a creative outlet for artistic minds and showcase talents in visual and performing arts.

Events: Art Exhibitions, Poster Making, Craft Workshops, Face Painting.

Eligibility: Open to students interested in creativity, design, and fine arts.

Technocrats - Coding & Robotics Club

Mission: Encourage innovation and problem-solving through coding, robotics, and tech competitions.

Events: Hackathons, Coding Challenges, Robotics Demos.

Eligibility: Open to all tech enthusiasts and engineering minds.

Rangmanch - Drama & Theatre Club

Mission: Promote performing arts and provide a platform for theatrical talent and expression.

Events: Skits, Street Plays, Mime Shows, Acting Workshops.

Eligibility: Open to students with a flair for performance and storytelling.
